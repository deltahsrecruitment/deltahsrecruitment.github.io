What is STEM

S: science

T: technology

E: engineering

M: mathmatics

Science

For the first three years at Delta we focus on physics, chemistry, biology, and health. Some of the past projects related to these topics are the bio bottle which is a project that focused on trying to make a small scaled ecosystem.

Technology

One of the tech based courses at Delta is Microsoft Office Specialist in which students learn how to properly use Word, PowerPoint, and Excel and get certifications in them too. Another tech course is Computer Science where they learn the basics of coding, which is delved deeper into in Multimedia Web Design.

Engineering

We take at Delta multiple engineering classes one of those classes is Pre-engineering in which we learn the basics of designing things in CAD software. Some of the things that are done in this course are designing and building a bridge along with designing a Lego.

Math

Delta offers many math courses from Integrated Math to Calculus, Multivariable Calculus, and Finite Math.
