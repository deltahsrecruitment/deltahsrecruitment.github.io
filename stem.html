What is STEM

S: science

T: technology

E: engineering

M: mathmatics

Science

For the first three years at Delta we focus on physics, chemistry, biology, and health. Some of the past projects related to these topics are the bio bottle which is a project that focused on trying to make a small scaled Ecosystem.

Technology

One of the tech based courses at Delta is Microsoft user specialist in which students learn how to properly use word, powerpoint and excel and try to get their certifications in them to. Another tech course is computer science where they learn the basics of coding which is delved deeper into in multimedia web design.

Engineering

We take at Delta multiple engineering classes one of those classes is Pre-engineering in which they learn the basics of designing things in CAD software. Some of the things that are done in this course are designing and building a bridge along with designing a Lego.
