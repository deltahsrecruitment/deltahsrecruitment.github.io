Delta has a variety of clubs for all students.

Club Description
Delta Senate Delta Senate are the student leaders of Delta High School.
Ambassadors Delta Student Ambassadors serve to represent our school at events both on & off campus.
Bible Club Delta's Bible Club meets weekly to discuss passages from the bible and how they relate to everyday student life.
Cyber Patriots The CyberPatriots members spend the first half the school year training and competing in the national CyberPatriots Program put on by the US Air Force.
Delta & Dragons Delta & Dragons is a tabletop gaming club for Delta students.
eSports Club eSports Club is based off of the new competitive world of gaming.
Fine Arts The Delta Fine Arts Club enjoys spending time together in creative pursuits.
International Language Club Students in the International Language Club have interests in learning and studying other cultures and countries.
Key Club The Delta Key Club is a service organization affiliated with a local Kiwanis chapter.
Literary Club Delta’s Literary Club (formerly known as the Creative Writing Club) meets together to share writing tips, review each other’s work, share about our favorite books, and to provide a place for people to practice their craft.
Movie Club Movie Club takes an hour after school to relax and watch movies.
National Honor Society The Delta National Honor Society is a service organization committed to scholarship, service, leadership and character.
Robotics Delta's Robotics Club is a competitive team made up of students from all grade levels. They are dedicated to building robots and competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge.
Science Olympiad Science Olympiad is an international nonprofit organization devoted to improving the quality of science education, creating a passion for learning science and providing recognition for outstanding achievement in science education by both students and teachers.

We Are Delta WE R Delta is a peer mentoring and leadership group whose purpose is to be provided with training, resources, and opportunity to make a positive difference in the school, local, and global communities.
Yearbook The Delta Media Group designs the school yearbook.
