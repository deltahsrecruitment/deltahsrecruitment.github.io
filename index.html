What is Delta?

Delta is a school that focuses on projects and collaboration between students. This collaboration is to set up the students to feel what a workplace environment would feels like. Along with the focus on collaboration Delta also offers college courses, and internships.

College Courses

There are a plethora of college courses at Delta and even more being planned out to be added. Some of the college courses that are offered right now are Pre-calculus, calculus, sociology 101, and some ELA.

Internships

At Delta in advisory we prepare students for the future in the workplace, for freshman we try to learn what they want to do in the future, in sophomore year we focus on building some real world skills, then in junior year we focus on the actual internships with things like mock interviews.
